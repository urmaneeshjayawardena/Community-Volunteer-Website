About nexSErve

Connecting people to services, building stronger communities

Our Mission

nexSErve was founded to bridge the gap between people in need and the services available to help them. We believe that access to healthcare, education, transportation, and social services should be available to everyone.

How We Help

Our free online directory brings together trusted local organizations in one easy-to-use platform. With simple category filtering and clear contact information, finding the right help is just a few clicks away.

Our Commitment

We verify all services listed in our directory and update information regularly. All services listed are either free or low-cost. We do not charge organizations to be listed or users to access information.
